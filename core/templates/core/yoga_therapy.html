Yoga Therapy is a method of natural body healing, being one of the forms of alternative medicine. The treatment of ailments is carried out through yoga practices, such as asanas (postures), pranayama (breathing exercises), and concentration techniques.

In our practice we employ an anatomical - physiological concept.

Yoga Therapy can serve as both a primary and auxiliary method of treatment aiming to improve the quality of life, alleviate side effects of pharmaceuticals and normalize overall well-being and mood.

Methods of Yoga Therapy include:

  • Asanas (static body positions), dynamic joint exercises and overall body strengthening, vinyasas (dynamic exercises focused on entering and exiting asanas).
  • Pranayamas (breathing practices) encompassing methods for:
    • optimizing external breathing: nose, bronchi, lungs; optimizing capillary flow; expropriating blood from the reserves in internal organs
    • optimizing internal tissue respiration within the cell.
  • Mudras, bandhas: impact on the nervous system.
  • Relaxation techniques.
