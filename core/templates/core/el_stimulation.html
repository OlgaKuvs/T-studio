DENAS-Vertebra is a medical device designed for electrical stimulation enabling the implementation of four treatment programs targeting dorsal areas. The device sends sets of electrical impulses, modulating them to deliver a massage to the human neural system in a language it understands. Through repeated messaging, the neural system reacts, regulating local metabolism to address bodily issues. DENAS Vertebra can be highly beneficial in managing chronic pain.

Program (A) works on the entire back, revitalizing the organism and strengthening the immune system. This can lead to a reduction in infectious diseases, alleviation of autoimmune diseases, and improved adaptation to physical and stressful loads.

Program (B) focuses on specific back areas, providing a pain-soothing effect for acute issues such as injuries, bruises, radicular syndrome, and fresh intervertebral hernias.

Program (C) addresses moderate and mild back pain, exacerbation of chronic internal organ diseases, old intervertebral hernias, bulging discs, degenerative disc disease, and any chronic trunk pain.

Program (D) is intended for emergency aid in cases of acute local back pain and can be combined with programs B or C for enhanced results.

Each treatment session lasts 25 minutes, individually adjusted for comfort and even pleasure. DENAS Vertebra has minimal contraindications and no side effects. The cost of each session is low, considering the recommended long-term treatment of 14-28 sessions. This medical device is suitable for use by adults and children over the age of 12.

Examples of DENAS Vertebra application include:

  • Improvement of general well-being and normalization of disturbed sleep and mood.
  • Fast recovery after injuries, diseases, and surgical interventions.
  • Prevention of various illnesses and recurrence of chronic internal diseases of the heart, lungs, and gastrointestinal tract.
  • Prevention, treatment, and management of diseases of the musculoskeletal system
