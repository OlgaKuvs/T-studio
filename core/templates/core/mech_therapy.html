In our arsenal of mechanotherapy, the following mechanical methods of therapeutic impact on the human body are available:

  • Application of needle applicators
  • Joint traction of the legs and spine on an inversion table

Joint traction of the legs and spine on an Inversion Table. An inversion table is a free-standing, stable structure on the floor with a rotating surface on axes, allowing the fixation of the legs around the ankles. When turning, the head ends up below the level of the legs, and the rotation can reach almost a vertical position. The tilt angle is adjusted by the therapist, and the procedure duration is based on the patient's well-being.

Traction occurs under the influence of gravity. The intensity of traction is determined by the angle of inclination, the duration, and the degree of muscle relaxation of the patient. Sensations during the procedure vary from pleasant to discomfort due to foot compression in the fixator or a feeling of pressure in the head due to the unusual inverted position.

The duration of the procedure is 5-10 minutes, and the number of sessions can go up to 15. It has indications and contraindications. For effective self-use at home, a brief instruction is required.

Multi-needle applicators that contribute to more progressive recovery and health enhancement. The applicator needles are made of 5 different metals and are designed to impact the human skin. The base is crafted from natural medical rubber.

Applicators are produced in various modifications, such as pads, rollers, applicative belts, applicative tapes, and are an original, powerful device with numerous health-improving therapeutic possibilities.

Application therapy has a pronounced analgesic and antispasmodic effect. It improves blood circulation, lymphatic flow, microcirculation, reduces tissue swelling, activates tissue mechanisms of immune defence, increases the levels of endogenous opioid peptides and anti-stress hormones in the blood, lowers the sensitivity of pain receptors, has a positive psychoemotional impact, and, as a result, stimulates general adaptive mechanisms in humans. When the applicator needles come into contact with the skin, they stimulate the production of hormones and other substances that alter the metabolism of the individual, activating their "inner doctor."

Applicators can be used by adults and children aged 12 and above. The duration of a therapeutic session varies from 15 to 30 minutes in each specific case. You will go through three phases based on your sensations: 2-3 minutes of discomfort of varying intensity, a neutral phase, and relaxation. Positive emotions and the beneficial effect greatly compensate for the short unpleasant sensations at the beginning of the session.
